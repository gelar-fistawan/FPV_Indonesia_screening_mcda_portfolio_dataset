# Limitations for reuse

These files support national screening and portfolio-planning analysis only.

Do not interpret the results as:
- site-level engineering feasibility,
- permitting approval,
- final grid interconnection feasibility,
- bathymetric suitability,
- social or ecological acceptability,
- investment recommendation.

Important limitations:
1. Power infrastructure is represented by a power-plant proximity proxy, not official PLN transmission/substation distance.
2. Capacity scenarios are based on uniform area and density assumptions.
3. WDPA overlap is only a screening flag.
4. Natural lakes in the top ranks require ecological, fisheries, cultural, tourism, and local-governance assessment.
5. Stable-water thresholds require sensitivity analysis before final policy use.
